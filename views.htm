Elect Craig Klatzco

The fact that I live work, shop, bank, and have educated my children in Lincolnwood has given me a unique and comprehensive view of our village. I would like residents to know I serve the interests of all members of our community, not just one specific group.

As trustee, I have observed fiscal responsibility, promoted economic development with a long-term vision, kept our taxes and fees low, and continued to improve relations with the School and Library Districts. I want to support local businesses, and serve the needs of our diverse population to the best of my ability.

The face of Lincolnwood has changed over the years. I am fortunate to be running for re-election with Mayor Gerald Turry, Ron S. Cope and Jean Ikezoe-Halevi. These candidates have the knowledge and experience to continue improving our infrastructure, promotng economic development, and celebrating the diversity in our village. We are at a important time in our town and need leaders that are objective, open to new ideas, and willing to work together for the improvement of our community. I have had the opportunity to work with these candidates and I feel they are the best choice for Lincolnwood.

As Village Trustee, my experience, education, knowledge, insight, and most of all my enthusiasm will help to continue improving the Village of Lincolnwood for all of our residents.
