Elect Craig L.Klatzco

 

 


The fact that I live work, shop, bank, and educate my children in Lincolnwood has given me a unique and comprehensive view of our village. I would like residents to vote for me because they share my vision of a better Lincolnwood.

As trustee, I want to better support local businesses, observe fiscal responsibility, execute economic development with a long-term vision with a goal to, lower our taxes and fees, and continue to improve relations with the School and Library Districts.

Lincolnwood has changed over the years, but is in need of new views and ideas to bring about positive change in our village. We can no longer have the status quo. I have had the opportunity to objectively observe the interactions between the residents, the businesses and our local government.

This is why I believe I am a viable choice for Village Trustee. My experience, education, knowledge, insight, and most of all my enthusiasm will help bring positive change to our community if I am elected to the Village Board.
