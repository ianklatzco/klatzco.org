Elect Craig Klatzco

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

I am 58 years old and have been a Lincolnwood resident for over 23 years. My wife Lucyna and I have two children, Ian (16 years) and Eric (14 years), who always make us proud and never cease to amaze us. They have been well educated by our School District 74 schools and they learned responsibility, leadership, and civic duty from our local Boy Scout chapter, where I am a Scout Leader.

My family businesses, Novelty Golf and the Bunny Hutch at Devon & Lincoln, have been a part of Lincolnwood for over 60 years. These family-focused entertainment businesses have allowed me to meet many residents and see children bring in their children a generation later.
I am fortunate in that Lincolnwood has been a part of my life in many ways.

The Klatzco Family has a tradition of supporting our community.

We are members of the Friends of the Lincolnwood Library. We support both the local PTA and the DARE anti-drug program in our local schools.

We have sponsored:

  • Lincolnwood Girls Softball
  • Lincolnwood Youth Baseball
  • The Annual Easter Easter Egg Hunt
  • The Annual Turkey Trot
  • The Fraternal Order of Police
  • The Paramedics and Fire Department
  • The Lincolnwood Chamber of Commerce
  • The Library Summer Reading Program.

These are just a few of the community programs in which we are involved.
